Common Phishing Red Flags

  • Urgency or Threats: "Act now or your account will be closed!"
  • Suspicious Sender: Check the email address carefully (e.g., support@paypa1.com).
  • Mismatched Links: Hover over links to see the real URL.
  • Unexpected Attachments: Never open unknown files (zip, exe, etc.).
  • Bad Grammar/Spelling: Professional companies rarely make obvious mistakes.
  • Requests for Info: Legitimate companies won't ask for passwords via email.
